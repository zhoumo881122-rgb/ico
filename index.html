What this favicon generator creates

A complete favicon setup should include favicon.ico for legacy browsers, PNG icons for modern tabs and pinned shortcuts, an Apple Touch Icon for iOS, and a 512x512 web app icon for installable sites.

Private by design

Your image is processed locally in your browser. It is not uploaded to a server, which keeps logo drafts and brand assets under your control.

Best favicon tips

Use a simple, high-contrast mark with generous padding. Avoid tiny text, thin outlines and photographic detail because favicons are often displayed at 16x16 pixels.

Favicon FAQ

What image format can I upload?

You can upload PNG, JPG, WebP or SVG files. Transparent PNG and SVG artwork usually produce the cleanest favicon results.

Which favicon sizes should I use?

Use 16x16, 32x32 and 48x48 inside favicon.ico, plus 180x180 for Apple Touch Icon and 192x192 or 512x512 for web app icons.

Do I need to install anything?

No. The generator runs in a modern browser and creates downloadable files directly on your device.
